Princeton EE Graduate Admissions (December 15th Deadline)

If you are a senior undergraduate or have completed your undergraduate degree, and you enjoy this course, we encourage you to apply to Princeton University's Electrical Engineering Department. We have many great researchers in Computer Engineering and are actively seeking excellent Ph.D. candidates for the next academic year. The application deadline is fast approaching (December 15th). You can find more information about Princeton Electrical Engineering and about how to apply at the following link:

Final Exam

Dates and instructions for the final exam have been posted. The final exam will be released on Friday, December 5 at 11:00 pm EST. It will be due Friday, December 19 at 11:00 pm EST, and the peer assessments will begin one hour following. Peer assessments are due on Friday, January 9, 2015 at 11:00 pm. The schedule and exam pages have been updated.
